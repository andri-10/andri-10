## 👋 About Me

I am a **Full Stack Developer** from Tirana, Albania, focused on building scalable web applications, backend services, and RESTful APIs.

Currently, I work as a **Full Stack Developer at Lufthansa Industry Solutions**, contributing to enterprise-level software solutions within the **Volkswagen ecosystem**.

My main stack includes **Java Spring Boot, Hibernate/JPA, MySQL, REST APIs, JavaScript, Angular, React, Python, Docker, Git, and Linux**.

I also have around **3 years of IT Operations experience**, supporting SAP systems and enterprise applications, which helped me build a strong foundation in troubleshooting, documentation, enterprise workflows, and production support.

I hold a **Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering** and I am currently pursuing a **Master of Science in Systems of Systems Engineering**.
